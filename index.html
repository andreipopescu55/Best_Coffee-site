1. Best Espresso

Espresso is a small, strong shot of coffee. It's the base for most coffee drinks. It has a powerful flavor and a layer of golden cream on top.

2. Best Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso with steamed milk and a thick layer of milk foam. It has a balanced taste and a rich, foamy texture.

12.99 RON

3. Best Latte

A latte is an espresso with a lot of steamed milk and just a little bit of foam. It has a mild coffee flavor and a smooth, milky taste.

12.99 RON

4. Best Macchiato

A macchiato is a shot of espresso with a small "stain" of milk foam. Its taste is strong, like coffee, but the milk foam balances it out slightly.

12.99 RON
